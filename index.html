Three Powerful AI Tools

Everything you need to land your next role — intelligent, fast, and purpose-built for job seekers.

📊
Resume Analyzer
  • Evaluates your resume against a specific job description
  • Checks ATS compatibility and job alignment score
  • Extracts and compares keywords and required skills
  • Identifies missing skills and experience gaps
  • Delivers AI-powered, actionable improvement suggestions
🔄
Resume Converter
  • Converts your general resume into a job-specific version
  • Rewrites the summary and aligns skills to the target role
  • Injects relevant keywords from the job description
  • Reorders sections based on job importance
  • Refines bullet points for stronger hiring impact
💬
Interview Q Prediction
  • Generates role-specific questions using RAG technology
  • Extracts key technical areas from the job description
  • Covers technical, HR, and scenario-based questions
  • Includes coding challenges where applicable
  • Optionally provides AI-generated sample answers
